"The Boy And The Heron"

Hayao Miyazaki’s latest masterpiece invites audiences into a fantastical world filled with wonder and discovery.

In a time when the boundary between reality and imagination blurs, a young boy embarks on a mysterious journey after encountering a majestic heron. Guided by the mythical creature, he uncovers secrets of a hidden world where nature and spirits coexist in fragile harmony. As he navigates challenges and uncovers truths about his own past, the boy learns about courage, loss, and hope.

In cinemas from July 14, 2023 for a limited engagement — don’t miss this magical adventure.
